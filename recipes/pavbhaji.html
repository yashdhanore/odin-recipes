Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food. Pav Bhaji is made with potatoes and vegetables cooked in a tomato base, and enjoyed with pav or dinner rolls. We will make it in both Instant Pot and Stovetop Pressure Cooker. Try this one-pot Pav Bhaji recipe in the pressure cooker and you will not make it any other way again!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Potatoes
  • Pepper
  • Cauliflower
  • Green Peas
  • Pav
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Soak red whole chili in 1 cup water for 15 mins. Grind chili, ginger and garlic along with the water to a paste.
  2. Start the pressure cooker in sauté mode (high flame for stovetop pressure cooker) and heat 2 tbsp butter/oil in it. Add onions and sauté for 3 minutes.
  3. Add bell pepper and sauté for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes, pav bhaji masala and salt. Add the ground chili paste. If anything is stuck to the bottom of the pot, scrape it with a spatula.
  5. Add cauliflower, potatoes, carrots, and green peas. Mix well.
  6. Open the pressure cooker and use an immersion blender to coarsely grind the ingredients. You can also use a potato masher to mash the bhaji to the desired consistency.
  7. Slice each pav or dinner rolls in two halves.
